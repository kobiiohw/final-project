STUDY OF LIGHT INTERACTING WITH SPACE

USING LIGHT TO AMPLIFY THE IDEA OF KNOWLEDGE BEING PASSED THROUGH DIFFERENT TYPES OF CONVERSATION. FILTERING LIGHT THROUGH THE STACKING AND SHIFTING OF MASSES ALLOWS FOR THE STORY OF KNOWLEDGE BEING PASSED THROUGH HIERARCHY OF SPACE. HERE THERE IS A LARGE VOID PIERCING THROUGH MASSES FOSSILIZED IN SPACE, TURNING THE MASSES INTO VOIDS THAT ALLOW FOR DIFFERENT CONVERSATIONS AND INTERACTIONS.
